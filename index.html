Welcome

Welcome to McKenzie Smith Voice Studio. Rooted in vocal wellness, artistic expression, and lifelong learning, my studio is a space where singers of all backgrounds can discover their most free, healthy, and expressive voice. Whether you're preparing for an audition, exploring your sound for the first time, or looking to deepen your technique, you’ll receive personalized instruction grounded in vocal anatomy, functional voice science, and joyful music-making. Let’s build a voice that feels as good as it sounds.

About Me

I'm McKenzie Smith — a voice teacher, choral conductor, and lifelong student of vocal health and artistry. My background includes classical training, choral directing, and a deep curiosity for how the voice works. I specialize in helping singers of all levels understand their instruments through functional technique, body awareness, and expressive freedom.

With a holistic approach and experience in both performance and education, I guide students toward confident, sustainable singing — whether they're preparing for the stage or singing just for joy.

Voice Studio

Lessons are tailored to each student's goals, vocal background, and learning style. I work with:

  • Beginner to advanced classical and contemporary singers
  • Audition and performance preparation
  • Functional vocal anatomy and technique
  • Choral singers looking to build solo skills

Weekly private lessons are available in-person and online. Contact me for current rates and availability.

My teaching combines technique, vocal health, artistic exploration, and goal-oriented structure to support each student's growth.
